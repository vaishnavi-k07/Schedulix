Setup Instructions

Before generating a timetable, ensure:

  1. At least one subject is added
  2. At least one teacher is registered with subject assignments
  3. At least one classroom is configured
  4. Time slots are defined for each working day

Generation Algorithm:

The system automatically considers:

  • Teacher availability windows
  • Classroom suitability and capacity
  • Subject type requirements
  • Break periods
  • Conflict-free scheduling

Tips:

  • Generation may take 30-60 seconds
  • Ensure sufficient time slots for all lectures
  • Review teacher workload limits
